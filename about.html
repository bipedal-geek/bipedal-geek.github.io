GeekBot A statically balancing Biped Robot

As from name we get little idea of what this project is? Basically this project is a Electrical Engineering 4rth Semester, subject Electronics Devices And Circuits Final Project. We started building this project 2 months ago. The idea of this project is based on adding easy solution to our lives and also innovating technology in Pakistan. Along with this motivation is to form better localized solutions for our country Pakistan in which we started with a cause of Do It Yourself Revolution.. Our project scrolls around two different systems described below.

BIPED - GEEK BOT

In simple words: GeekBot is a self-contained statically balance biped robot. The idea of such robot is to introduce new trend of robotics in Pakistan. This robot will walk on its foot-steps statically balance itself and avoid obstacles. The structure of this robot is based on 3D printed robot chassis. Basic need of building such robots is to localize the humanoid robots as it is one of the difficult field of robotics. As we need something more than derived vehicles.

Information Technology University



ITU, located in Lahore, came into being in 2012 in order to advance scholarship and innovation in the areas of science, technology and engineering. ITU aspires to emulate the success of Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) through focus on cross-disciplinary applied research, the cultivation of an entrepreneurial culture in teaching and research, the maintenance of a close collaboration with the Information and Communication Technology industry and the development of strong ties with government funding agencies. With a structure that combines the best features of both public and private sector universities, ITU aims to serve as a center for excellence in academia, research, entrepreneurship and innovation.
